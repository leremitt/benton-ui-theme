How can I book an appointment at your salon?

Booking an appointment at our salon is easy! You can either call us directly at our contact number or use our online booking system available on our website. Simply select the service you want, choose a date and time, and provide your contact details. We will confirm your appointment via email or phone.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made within less than 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment for your convenience. This includes cash, major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and mobile payment options such as Apple Pay and Google Pay. We do not accept checks at this time.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer gift certificates that make perfect gifts for any occasion. You can purchase them in various denominations directly from our salon or through our website. Gift certificates can be used for any service or product at our salon and are valid for one year from the date of purchase.
