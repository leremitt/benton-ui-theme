Engine Service

Car service involves a series of essential maintenance procedures designed to keep a vehicle in prime condition. These services include changing engine oil and filters, inspecting and rotating tires, checking and topping up fluid levels (such as coolant and brake fluid), and inspecting vital components like brakes, batteries, and lights.

How often should you have a car service done

Some modern vehicles are equipped with advanced technology that can adjust service intervals based on driving conditions and usage patterns. Additionally, if you notice any unusual sounds, vibrations, or changes in your vehicle's performance, it's advisable to have your car inspected by a mechanic regardless of the regular service schedule.

What are the benefits of having a car service done?

  • Increased Safety: Regular maintenance ensures that critical components like brakes, tires, lights, and steering are in optimal condition, enhancing your vehicle's safety on the road.
  • Preventative Maintenance: Car service helps in identifying and addressing potential issues before they become major problems, preventing costly repairs down the line.
